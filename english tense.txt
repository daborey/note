
past tense
Simple past: She walked to the park yesterday.
Past continuous: I was watching TV when the phone rang.
Past perfect: They had finished dinner before the guests arrived.
Past perfect continuous: We had been studying for hours before the exam.

future tense
Simple future: She will finish her homework by 6 pm.
Future continuous: They will be playing football this afternoon.
Future perfect: We will have completed the project by the end of the month.
Future perfect continuous: I will have been waiting for her for two hours by the time she arrives.

present
Simple present: She walks to school every day.
Present continuous: They are watching a movie right now.
Present perfect: I have lived in this city for five years.
Present perfect continuous: We have been waiting for the bus for half an hour.
